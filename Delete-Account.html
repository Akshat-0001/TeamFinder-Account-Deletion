How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your TeamFinder account and associated data, you can do so directly from within the app. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the TeamFinder app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (usually found in your profile or menu)
  3. Scroll down and tap on Advanced Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Profile Information: Your name, email, bio, skills, interests, and profile picture
  • Teams: Teams you created (if you're the owner) or your membership in teams
  • Messages: All chat messages you've sent within teams
  • Applications: Your applications to join teams
  • Notifications: All your notification history
  • Authentication Data: Your login credentials and session information
  • Device Tokens: Push notification tokens associated with your account

Data Retention Period

Account deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation. Your data is permanently removed from our active database within 24 hours.

Note: Backup copies in our disaster recovery systems may be retained for up to 30 days for technical and security purposes, after which they are permanently deleted.

What Happens After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will be immediately logged out of the app
  • You will no longer be able to access your profile or teams
  • Other users will no longer see your profile or messages
  • You can create a new account with the same email if you wish to return

Alternative: Temporary Deactivation

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, consider simply logging out of the app. Your account will remain inactive but can be reactivated anytime by logging back in.
